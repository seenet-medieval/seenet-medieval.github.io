--- title: "Call for Proposals" layout: default navigation_weight: 6 ---

The Society for Early English & Norse Electronic Texts currently welcomes publication proposals from scholars working on digital editions of texts in Old and Middle English, as well as electronic resources and reference works related to Old and Middle English materials. Interested editors should be familiar with XML- and TEI-based encoding procedures for medieval manuscript books.

Please send a brief letter of inquiry to the SEENET editorial board at editors@seenet.org.
